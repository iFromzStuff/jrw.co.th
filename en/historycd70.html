Company & History
JRW Utility Plc. (“JRW”) was founded by Mr. Jaran Wiwatjetsadawut on 2 November 1993 with Bt1 million in registered capital to provide the total telecommunication solutions that includes designing, supplying, installing, testing, consultancy as well as repair and maintenance services. JRW has been growing up well upon the customers’ trusts and hence increased revenue since its inception.

The Company registered its incorporation on 2 November 1993 under the name JRW Utility Co. Ltd. with Bt1 million in registered and paid-up capital for 10,000 ordinary shares, Bt100 par value each. Initially done were the subcontracting works about the constructing of signal cables, fiber and copper wires including towers, all of which were the Outside Plant (OSP) works, and  preparation of the equipment-installed site.
The registered and paid up capital were increased for several times to Bt75 million in 2003.
Jaya Soft Vision Co. Ltd. was jointly set up in January 2006 to run the online game business with Bt5 million in registered capital, divided into 500,000 ordinary shares at Bt10 par value. The Company held 225,000 shares or 45 per cent.
The head office was relocated from the address 126/235 Village no. 8, Watcharaphon Lane, Ramintra Road, Bangkok, to the 5-storey office at 32/288-290 Village no. 8, Ramintra 65 Lane, Ramintra Road, Bangkok, so as to expand the continually growing businesses.
The registered and paid up capital edged up to Bt108 million on 25 May 2007 thru issuance of 330,000 shares at Bt100 with the objectives of (1) paying off the borrowings to the Directors, and (2) spending for the business expansion.
More investment was made into Jaya Soft Vision Co. Ltd. in June 2007 by purchasing 500,000 new ordinary shares at Bt10 par value each. That increased the investment to 72.50 per cent of its registered and paid up capital.
The contract of sole exchange equipment distributor with Nokia Siemens Networks (Thailand) was signed in September 2007.
The joint investment to set up JRW Network Solutions Co. Ltd. was carried in September 2007 to maneuver the telecommunication system design and installation at Bt20 million in the registered capital, divided into 2 million ordinary shares at Bt10 par each. The Company held 0.6 million shares or 30 per cent of the registered and paid up capital.
The arch businesses were diverted from the subcontracting works of tower and cable system installing to the labor intensive designing, selling and installing of the telecommunication system under the cooperation with JRW Network Solutions Co. Ltd. which were skilled in the field. In February 2008, the Company started to do the designing works and selling the core control equipment from TOT Plc. and acquired 6 contracts of the turnkey and installing of the equipment in main exchange plans for the amount of Bt223.62 million.

The registered and paid up capital climbed up to Bt159 million in February 2008 by issuance of 0.51 million new shares at Bt100 par with the aim of (1) purchasing the land in Lam Lukka district, Pathumthani Province, to erect the warehouse for business expansion, (2) paying off borrowings to Directors, and (3) spending it on business expansion. The increase in the paid up capital was registered at the Ministry of Commerce on 7 February 2008.
Jaya Soft Vision Co. Ltd. increased its registered capital by issuing the new 0.5 million shares at Bt10 par value each. The Company did not make purchase and hence the investment in that firm dipped to 48.33 per cent of the registered and paid up capital.
The Company signed the Contract of 1996’s SPC Equipment Replacement with TOT Plc. for the amount of Bt232.49 million and the 300-day working period in January 2009.
JRW Utility Network Solution Co. Ltd., a joint company, decreased its registered and paid up capital from Bt20 million to Bt10 million, which cut down the ordinary shares held by the Firm to 0.3 million, Bt10 each, and the Company received the share amount from such decrease on 27 April 2009.
The Company adjusted its main business structure from the subcontracting works of tower and cable system installing to the total telecommunication solutions of the designing, supplying, installing, testing and consultancy services in April 2009. The investment in JRW Network Solutions Co. Ltd. was sold to the existing shareholders at the price of Bt10 per share and its engineering teams with skills in the telecommunication solution design were transferred to the Company. That company was later changed its name to Information & Communication Networks Co. Ltd. Thus, the Company has not had any connection with that firm after the share sale.
The contract with Samart Telecom  Plc. (“Samart”) and Jasmin Telecom Systems Plc. (“Jasmin”) was signed with the Company on May 2009 to carry out the installation of IP Broadband equipment for TOT Plc. in the amount of Bt278.86 million. The entire project was worth Bt3,995 million.
The entire investment in Jaya Soft Vision Co. Ltd. was sold in June 2009 to the existing shareholders at Bt10 par per share, making the earning Bt7.25 million for the Company. Since then, the Company has not had any undertaking with that firm.
On 3 August 2009, the shareholders’ extraordinary meeting no. 3/2552 granted the following significant resolutions.
1. Approving the alteration of the par value of the Company’s share from Bt100 to Bt1, hence
    making its registered capital to Bt159 million divided into 159 million shares. The change was
    registered at the Ministry of Commerce on 11 August 2009.
2. Permitting the conversion from being a limited company to a public limited company. The
    registration was carried out at the Ministry of Commerce on 11 August 2009.
3. Giving consent to the plan to issue and sell 53 million new ordinary shares with Bt1 in par value
    to the general public.
4. Granting green light to the listing of the corporate shares in the Securities Exchange MAI
The Company signed the Hybrid Aggregate Switch Installing Contract with TOT Plc. for Bt186 million in value in August 2009.
The equipment sale contract and the working system installing contract were signed with TOT Plc. and the equipment repair and maintenance contract with a governmental office  for the amount of Bt101.83 million.
The Company signed the exchange plant equipment system installing contract with TOT Plc. and other three contracts with the private firms, totaling 8 contracts for Bt67.29 million.

As at 31 March 2010, the Company recorded Bt212 million in registered capital, divided into 212 ordinary stakes with Bt1 par value each, of which Bt159 was paid up.
