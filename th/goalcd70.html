Goal
Below are the corporate operating goals.
Keeping the important customer bases, which are TOT Plc., CAT Telecom, relevant government and private organizations to assure their continued service uses with the focus on maximum customer satisfaction from product or service purchases.
Expanding and diversifying the business scopes in line with the increased corporate skills and experiences so as to disperse risks of relying on one main customers such as designing, supplying and installing equipment within the exchange plants other than the core control equipment, project works as per the government’s future telecommunication system development plans like the 3G Project, the Next Generation Network Project (NGN) and the works of the private sector.
Increasing new business allies which are equipment suppliers in order to enhance the competing potentials and sale opportunities.
Making higher operating efficiency through continued manpower capacity development to serve the future business expansion.
